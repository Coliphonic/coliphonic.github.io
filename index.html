Who am I?

Hello, my name is Colin, I'm an audio engineer and theatrical technican based in Phoenix, Arizona.

I have six years of experience in working in live sound, recording, theater and film. I have been nominated for an AriZoni Award for sound design as well as Best Sound Design and Original Composition from the SCC Film Festival.

Some of the venues I work for are the ASU Kerr Cultural Center, which is a division of ASU Gammage, The Nash: a jazz club in downtown Phoenix, and Paradise Valley Community College. I have also worked for theaters such as the Musical Instrument Museum, Phoenix Theatre and The Herberger Theater.
